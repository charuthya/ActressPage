Tamanna Santosh Bhatia (born 21 December 1989), known as Tamannaah Bhatia,[a] is an Indian actress who predominantly works in Telugu, Tamil and Hindi films. Having appeared in 89 films and ranking among the highest-paid actresses in South Indian cinema, she has received several awards, including three Santosham Film Awards, two SIIMA Awards and the Kalaimamani Award.
